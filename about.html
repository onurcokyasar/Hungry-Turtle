Our Story

Welcome to London's best Kurdish Charcoal grill restaurants. At the heart of London's well known Turtle Lane, we offer the best in Mediterranean cuisine. Always created with fresh, top quality meat, our kebabs, pizzas, and fresh salads bring you the taste and great value on which our family business has always based its success.

Come along and dine with us, whether you want a romantic night out for two or a larger space for a celebration or birthday party, we are set up to deliver a high value dining experience.
